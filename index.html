About

Welcome to the Mathematical Society! The goal of our organization is to make progress in spreading mathematics education through the community through social events, tutoring, and fostering undergraduate research. We believe in fostering a environmentally-friendly and colloborative system that will address the needs of the Rio Grande Community and the students / faculty of UTRGV.
